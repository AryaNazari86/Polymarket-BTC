⑦ Strategy Philosophy
🤖 What Other Bots Do
Algorithmic bots analyze BTC OHLCV,
momentum indicators (RSI, EMA, MACD)
and historical patterns to predict
whether BTC closes UP or DOWN in
the 5-minute window.

They bid up Polymarket odds based
on their model's confidence score.
📈 What ARYA Does
ARYA doesn't predict BTC price.

It watches what those bots predict
by observing how odds drift in the
first 75 seconds.

If odds are moving decisively in one
direction AND BTC confirms it → ride
the wave. Sell before it peaks.
💡 Why This Works
Odds momentum is self-reinforcing.
Rising Up odds → more bots buy Up
→ odds rise further → peak near s180.

We capture the middle of that move:
enter at s80 (momentum confirmed),
exit at s180 (before crowd locks in).

BTC as filter eliminates false signals.
